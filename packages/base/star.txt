How to view map;
Mac:
1. Extract files to Users/Your Name/Library/Application Support/sauerbraten/packages/base

2. Open Sauerbraten and type "/map star” (without quotation marks) Or open the .ogz file which will open Sauerbraten for you and load up the map.

Windows:
1. Extract files to C:/Program Files/Sauerbraten/packages/base

2. Open Sauerbraten and type "/map star” (without quotation marks)
